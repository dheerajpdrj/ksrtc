Passenger who Travelling to KARNATAKA From KERALA

As per the Karnataka State Government order, The mandate of possessing RT-PCR negative test reports was discontinued for passengers arriving from Kerala. All passengers coming from Kerala State to Karnataka will mandatorily carry the two-dose / full dose vaccination certificate. This Must be submitted to the officials if necessary.

Passenger who Travelling to KERALA From Other States

As per the Kerala State Govt. orders, all passengers coming to Kerala from other states shall mandatorily produce a negative RT-PCR certificate that is not older than 72 hours or vaccination certificate of two dose of COVID 19 vaccine. This Must be presented when requested by the conductor.
